REGISTRAR · IDENTITY REGISTRY

Enroll bulk identities.

An authoritative identity registry based on a core functional or foundational ID e.g. a national ID repository. Plug in your data source (CSV, secured API, database, or Sunbird RC), map its columns to the identity fields, and enroll citizens. Holders later activate against these records via eSignet; they cannot create themselves.
